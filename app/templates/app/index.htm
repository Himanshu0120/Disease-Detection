Overview

In this webapp we hve tried to extend the role of IT in the medical field
Here a patient can check whether he is having a disease or not.The patient has to provide some of his details related to the disease to get the computed results.
Here the patients can get the results immediately and also without paying any fee.
It also helps in reducing the dependency on the doctors.

About

The app can dectect various diseases like cancer,malaria,pneumonia,etc based on the data provided by the user. It uses various machine learning and deep learning models to detect the disease.These models have been trained on real world data and are expexted to provide results with good accuracies.
